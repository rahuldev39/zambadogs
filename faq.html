FAQ's for Dog Host

This is a wonderful opportunity for dog lovers to make some money and at the same time, enjoy the company of loving, furry friends for their preferred period of time!

Anyone who loves dogs and has experience caring for them- currently or in the past, can become a host.

If you don’t have any previous experience, we recommend you begin with one of our short-term options to see how comfortable you are. You could start by signing up for being a Day Care provider or a Fun Buddy first, before taking responsibilities for a longer period of time.

You can start hosting in three easy steps:

  • Express your willingness by signing up under our ‘Become a Host’ section.
  • Fill up your details in the form.
  • Have an at-home meeting with our Customer Care Executive.

He will verify the information you shared with us, as well as share tips on how you can appropriately host dogs in your home. Verification would also assure you of your upcoming hosting experience and guarantees the dog parents of your readiness to host their dog.

Once your hosting period has ended, we will transfer the amount to you within 4 days, using your preferred payment method, after deducting a 15% service charge. The service charge is towards running the business and attracting more customers to enrol with Zamba dogs so that you may get more business in the future. In case of any delay please reach out to our Customer care executive.

In case there is a medical emergency, the dog parent(s) should be informed of the same at the earliest. You should reach out to the Vet recommended by them when they dropped their dog off. In case you are unable to reach out to the recommended Vet, our 24X7 Woof Care team will help you with other options in your area. In case of any other emergency, please inform the Dog parent and the emergency contact that has been provided. You may also please contact our Woof Care team for further assistance.

We understand you may need to cancel a reservation due to some unforeseen circumstances. But this may mean a major disruption for the dog parent as he/she would have made travel plans based on your confirmation. Under such circumstances, we request you to ensure the following:

  • Cancel the booking on the calendar so that the dog parent and we get to know of the cancellation.
  • Cancellations prior to a week will allow us to make alternate arrangements.
  • Cancellation with notice of fewer than three days is liable to attract a penalty of Rs.250/- for Cozy Homes and Rs.50/- for other services, along with a negative rating. The amount will be deducted from your account.
  • Cancellation with less than a day will attract a penalty of 1 days service and will be deducted from your account. Repeat offenders will be blacklisted.

FAQ’s for Dog Parents

This is a wonderful opportunity for dog lovers to make some money and at the same time, enjoy the company of loving, furry friends for their preferred period of time!

Three key reasons why you should think of us:

  • We work only with dog lovers. All our hosts are verified for authenticity and reliability by our team to ensure that they are capable of being loving, have adequate experience with taking care of dogs and are certified by us in elementary dog care.
  • We try our best to establish the best possible relationship between you and potential dog hosts who would love to open their homes and hearts to your dog.
  • To facilitate a totally dependable support system for your dog, our Woof Care team is always at the ready, 24x7, to lend a helping hand so that you can have a stress-free time away, knowing your dog is well looked after.

  • Register yourself by filling in your details on the Zamba Dogs website.
  • Go through the host profiles and select two or three potential hosts based on the services you need:
    • Cozy homes, for when you are away for an extended period of time.
    • Daycare for day-long services for more than 6 hours a day.
    • Fun Buddy for dog sitting services for up to 4-5 hours during the day or in the evening.
  • Whenever you need our services, check for your favourite host's availability on the calendar and place a request. (As is understandable, the earlier a notice you give, the better)
  • Once the host accepts your request, set up a meeting with him and your dog too. We strongly encourage you to meet the hosts at their homes before you plan ahead. This will reassure you of the safety of the environment and its congeniality.
  • Pay and confirm booking with Zamba Dogs. You can now relax in peace with the assurance that your dog will be well taken care of, while you are away.

Zamba Dogs goes to great lengths to ensure that only genuine dog lovers are part of our community. We have a stringent system in place to authenticate and verify hosts through an expert support team so that you can rest easy knowing that your dog is in safe hands. This support team keeps all boxes checked related to your dog's safety and well being. They also guide and coach the host families if needed.

However, despite the best efforts Zamba Dogs cannot guarantee the safety of the host's home. It is up to you to ensure that your host's arrangements are up to your satisfaction. Zamba Dogs will not be liable for any accidents/mishaps during the tenure of your dog's stay.

We strongly encourage you to meet the hosts at their homes before you plan ahead.

  • This will reassure you of the safety of the environment and its congeniality.
  • If the host too has a dog, this would be a good time to see if they'll get along.
  • This would also be an excellent opportunity to brief the host about your dog, especially regarding key aspects such as daily routines, walking, feeding, supplements, temperament and other health matters if any.
  • We would also encourage you to share your regular Vet’s contact details with the host.

All of us at Zamba Dogs want you to remain stress-free while you are away from your dog. Which is why we have procedures in place to tackle any given medical emergencies.

If one were to arise during the duration of your dog's stay, the host would inform you about the same and take him to the Vet of your recommendation. In case your Vet is not available the Zamba Dogs Woof Care team will help the host locate the closest option. We will also be contacting the person you've registered as an emergency contact so that they can have a first-hand understanding of the situation.

The rates vary for different hosts, based on their ratings which are displayed in their profile. It also varies depending on the facilities provided, the experience of the host and reviews.

To confirm a reservation, you have to pay the total charges applicable through Zamba Dogs using a Credit Card, Net Banking or via Mobile wallets. We will settle the account with the host at the end of the stay.

We understand that plans may change and you may have to cancel a reservation. Under such circumstances, we encourage you to make the cancellation as early as possible as the hosts would have blocked dates exclusively for your dog.

Cancellation charges as indicated by the host and other finance charges would be deducted and the remaining amount would be refunded to you within 4 business days.
