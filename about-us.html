About Zamba Dogs

We Are Making Dog Ownership Easier For Everyone!.

Zamba Dogs plans to leverage cutting edge technology to provide the best dog care services just to keep dogs happy. Though the core of Zamba Dogs is a sophisticated technology engine, we combine it with a completely human interface to provide the best care for your dogs.

Roy, Zamba Dogs Founder and Top Dog, and his wife Rani faced a common issue among dog owners- not knowing where to leave their dog while they are travelling. Most dogs want human company and therefore don’t particularly like being left behind in kennels or boarding. Roy and Rani were lucky to find a dog-loving couple who was willing to keep their dog while they were away. Roy wanted to extend this facility to all dog parents and started Zamba Dogs. Since dogs are treated like children by their parents, he roped in Rani and dog lover mothers, Anjum and Nivedita, who were taking a break from their corporate careers, to help him set up Zamba Dogs.

Our Leadership

Roy Cherian

Founder Zambadogs

Roy is a huge dog lover and you will often find dogs following him wherever he goes- be it the golf course or the streets. He is a successful entrepreneur who set up Marketelligent, a data analytics company, which he sold a couple of years back. He spent most of his corporate life with Nestle and FCB Ulka.

Roy holds an MBA from IIM Ahmedabad and BTech from College of Engineering, Trivandrum.

Anjum Kapur

Anjum is an ardent dog lover, art enthusiast, travel blogger and enjoys swimming. She is a senior partnerships and communications professional with over two decades of corporate experience heading Partnerships at the British Council and managing major advertising accounts at McCann Erickson, Grey Worldwide and FCB Ulka.

Anjum holds an MBA from Indian Institute of Foreign Trade, New Delhi and is a Post Graduate in Mass Communication from the Indian Institute of Mass Communication.

Nivedita Rathore

Nivedita is a dog lover, dog magnet by nature... a wanderer by heart with an entrepreneurial spirit, full time Mom to two daughters and Bella, the resident diva cocker spaniel. She started her career in Sales & Marketing with the Taj group of hotels. She moved to Advertising Sales and held leadership positions in Star TV and ESPN Star.

Nivedita holds a degree in Hotel Management from IHM Mumbai.

Rani Cherian

Rani was scared of dogs till Hobbes, her cocker spaniel,entered her life. She is now a complete convert and often encourages other people to get a dog of their own. She loves discussions about the subconscious mind and quantum physics.
